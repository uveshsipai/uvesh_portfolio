About Me

About

Dynamic and dedicated Assistant Professor in Electrical Engineering with over 8 years of academic and research experience. Adept at fostering collaborative learning, mentoring future engineers, and driving innovation in academia and industry. Recognized for excellence in teaching, research, and innovation with multiple awards and honours.
